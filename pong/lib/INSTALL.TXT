Copy the files as follows to install JOGL and JOAL on your system:

Linux:
	cp linux-i386/*.so* /opt/java-1.4.2.10/jre/lib/i386
	cp *.jar /opt/sunjdk-1.4.2.10/jre/lib/ext

Mac OS X:
	cp macosx-universal/*.jnilib /System/Library/FORGOTTEN
	cp *.jar /System/Library/FORGOTTEN

Windows (not sure):
	copy *.jar "C:\Program Files\Java 1.4\jre\lib\ext
	copy win32/*.dll "C:\Program Files\Java 1.4\jre\bin
	execute OpenALwEAX.exe
